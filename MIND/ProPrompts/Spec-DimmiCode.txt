# Dimmi-Code ProPrompt Spec

Standalone ProPrompts are single `.md` cards under `MIND/ProPrompts`.

## Layout
id: short-slug (same as filename)
title: quick summary
role: actor or owner
status: todo | doing | done
links: optional comma list

---
Body text with instructions.

## Folders
queue/   – active work
done/    – finished cards
archive/ – long term store

## Footer Pointer
Legacy files keep a tiny footer pointing here:
```
/// === PROPROMPT:BEGIN ===
/// see: MIND/ProPrompts/queue/<id>.md
/// === PROPROMPT:END ===
```
