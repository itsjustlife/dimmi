# PREPROMPT

Loads or crafts PREPROMPT templates for session bootstrapping.

Steps:
1. PREPROMPT files live in `Templates/PREPROMPTS/`.
2. Layer 1 announces a preprompt; Layer 2 provides system rules.
3. Use `TEMPLATE-PREPROMPT.txt` when authoring new blocks.
4. After applying a preprompt, continue with `Start.txt` flow.
