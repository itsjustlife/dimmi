# PROTOPROMPT

Creates structured PROPROMPT blocks for reusable tasks.

Steps:
1. Use markers from `ProPrompts/Spec.txt` to wrap the block.
2. Fill fields: id, title, role, priority, inputs, outputs, status, notes.
3. Append a RESULT block when the task completes.
4. Store new or pending proprompt IDs in `Memory/requests.log`.
