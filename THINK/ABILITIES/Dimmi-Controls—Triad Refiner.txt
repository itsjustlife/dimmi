[PURPOSE]:
Provide natural-language control phrases and micro-UI conventions Dimmi should use
while running the Triad Refiner ability. Keeps phrasing consistent and compact.

[SCOPE]:
Covers start cues, selection, branching, knob changes, navigation, and exports.

[CONTINUATION & REFINEMENT PROMPT]:
Use these phrases exactly or near-identically for consistency. Keep them short and scannable.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# START CUES
• “Triad: <seed>”
• “Give me 3 options for <goal>”
• “Start Triad for <mode>: <seed>”
• “Refine this with three options: <text>”

# SELECTION
• “Select A/B/C”  |  “Select 1/2/3”
• “Select: ‘<handle>’”
• “Keep Original” (keep prior selection available)
• “Back” (go to previous round)
• “Undo” (revert last action)

# BRANCHING
• “Fork A as ‘<BranchName>’”
• “Switch to branch ‘<BranchName>’”
• “List branches”
• “Merge branch ‘<BranchName>’ into main” (summarize deltas, do not overwrite)

# KNOBS (examples)
• “Change Knobs: spread=wild, tone=playful, length=condense”
• “Focus on imagery; audience=kids; format=outline”
• “Add constraints: must include ‘urban’, avoid ‘pesticide’”
• “Reset knobs” (back to defaults)

# NAVIGATION
• “Show Path” (breadcrumb lineage with short reasons)
• “Show Options” (reprint most recent A/B/C)
• “Explain B” (1–2 line rationale for B)
• “Compare A vs C” (succinct comparison)

# EXPORTS
• “Export: json”
• “Export: opml”
• “Export: html”
• “Export: txt (compact)”

# FINISH
• “Finish” (lock selection & output final bundle)
• “Finish with summary” (add 5-bullet recap: what was chosen, why, next steps)
